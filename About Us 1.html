Who We Are

This terminal is maintained by anonymous survivors and whistleblowers dedicated to exposing the atrocities committed by bio-terrorism organizations worldwide.

Our mission is to archive and preserve the data from the Raccoon City Destruction Incident and subsequent global outbreaks. The world must know the truth about Umbrella, Tricell, Neo-Umbrella, and The Connections.
