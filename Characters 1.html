Leon S. Kennedy

DSO Agent

Former R.P.D. rookie cop whose first day on the job was the Raccoon City outbreak. Now a highly trained government agent working directly under the President.

Jill Valentine

BSAA SOA

Former S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team member and master of unlocking. A seasoned survivor of the Spencer Mansion incident and the nemesis pursuit in Raccoon City.

Chris Redfield

BSAA Captain

Former S.T.A.R.S. marksman, now a key founding member and captain in the BSAA. Dedicated his entire life to eradicating bio-terrorism across the globe.

Claire Redfield

TerraSave

Chris's younger sister. Came to Raccoon City looking for him and ended up surviving the nightmare alongside Leon. Now works with the NGO TerraSave.
