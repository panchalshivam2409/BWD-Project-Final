Known Survivors & Operatives

Leon S. Kennedy

Former R.P.D. rookie cop whose first day on the job was the Raccoon City outbreak. Now a highly trained government agent.

Jill Valentine

S.T.A.R.S. Alpha Team member and master of unlocking. A seasoned survivor of the Spencer Mansion incident.

Chris Redfield

Former S.T.A.R.S. marksman, now a key captain in the BSAA. Dedicated his life to eradicating bio-terrorism.

Claire Redfield

Chris's younger sister. Came to Raccoon City looking for him and ended up surviving the nightmare. Works with TerraSave.
